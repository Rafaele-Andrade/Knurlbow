# Knurlbow
Knurl bow inspired on the design of Fello, made by Andi Otto in collaboration with Nicolo Merendino


What you will need:

* Push button
* Accelerometer
* Led
* 2 resistors of 1k
* ESP32 or similiar
* li on battery to charge your microcontroller
